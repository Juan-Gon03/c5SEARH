**Our Goal:**  
Our goal with this website is to have it as a hub of information to inform people of issues they might have not known existed and include links to various other similar projects and nonprofits.

**What is Sex Ed:**  
Sexual education relating to human sexuality, anatomy, and activity 

**What is Reproductive Health:**  
Reproductive health is the mental, physical and emotional well being when it comes to intimacy, not only a focuses on disease
